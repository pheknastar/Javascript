MS Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, also known as MS Dhoni, is a legendary Indian cricketer and former captain of the India national cricket team. He is famous for his calm leadership, wicketkeeping, and finishing skills. Under his captaincy, India won the ICC Cricket World Cup, ICC World Twenty20, and ICC Champions Trophy. He also plays for Chennai Super Kings in IPL.
