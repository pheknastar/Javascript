Rohit Sharma

Rohit Sharma is an Indian cricketer and captain of the India national cricket team in major formats. He is known for his explosive batting and elegant stroke play. Rohit is famous for holding the record of the highest individual score in ODI cricket (264 runs) and for leading Mumbai Indians to multiple IPL titles.
