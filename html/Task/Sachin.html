Sachin Tendulkar

Sachin Tendulkar is a legendary Indian cricketer known as the “God of Cricket.” He is one of the greatest batsmen in cricket history and holds records for the most runs and most centuries in international cricket. Sachin played for the India national cricket team for over 20 years and won the ICC Cricket World Cup with India.
